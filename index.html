Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) connects everyday objects, like appliances and cars, to the internet using sensors. This makes them smart enough to collect data and automatically work together to make life easier and more efficient.

Robotics: Automation in Action

Robotics is the science of designing and building machines that can sense their environment and perform tasks. These machines, or robots, are programmed to automate complex jobs, making them faster, safer, and more precise than human labor.

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is the perfect starting point for learning electronics and coding. It's a circuit board that lets you easily connect components like sensors and motors and program them to interact with the physical world.
